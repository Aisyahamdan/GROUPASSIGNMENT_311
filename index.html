About Us


Tradition since 2015

Hasyaa Bakery is a Malaysian halal-certified café chain company established since 2015. It has international branches in Singapore and Indonesia. It serves desserts such as cakes, muffin, pastry, drinks and croissants as a main menu. As a leading and largest café chain in Malaysia with Halal-certification awarded by Jabatan Kemajuan Islam Malaysia (JAKIM). Hasyaa Bakery is the leading bakery café in Malaysia as well as in ASEAN is still expanding its wings with growing demands and customer. Hasyaa Bakery serves fine quality and delicious homemade cake and pastries together with fresh drinks of Asia dishes and beverages. Hasyaa Bakery is targeting for individuals, families and take out customers with higher income by selling high quality, innovative products at a moderate price, designing tasteful, convenient locations and providing high quality customer service.
